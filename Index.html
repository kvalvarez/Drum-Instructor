My name is JC Alvarez, and I've been dedicated to drumming since I was 10 years old. My musical journey began in worship drumming and has grown into a career encompassing both intimate studio sessions and dynamic live.
I was the drummer for Ransom Jack, whose music can still be found on Amazon and Spotify from 14 years ago. Between 2006 and 2012, I played over 600 shows across Texas, leaving my mark on renowned venues, from the iconic 6th Street in Austin to vibrant stages in San Antonio and Houston.
I am passionate about sharing my love for drumming through local and virtual classes. Whether you're just starting out or looking to refine your skills, let's create some incredible rhythms together!
